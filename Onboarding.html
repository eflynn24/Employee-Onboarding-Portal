🎉 Welcome to Our Team!

We're excited to have you join us! This onboarding portal will guide you through the important steps to get started. Please fill out your information and complete all the tasks to ensure a smooth transition.

Getting Started

Step 1: Start by providing your basic employment information on the "Employee Info" tab.

Step 2: Work through our onboarding checklist to ensure you have everything you need.

Step 3: Review helpful resources and documentation on the "Resources" tab.

Step 4: Check your summary to confirm all steps are complete.
